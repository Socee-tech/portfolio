About WildHaven Game Park

Nestled in the heart of nature, WildHaven Game Park is a sanctuary where wildlife thrives and adventure lives. For over 20 years, we've been a top destination for eco-tourists, families, and nature enthusiasts.

Our Story

WildHaven was established in 2005 with a mission to preserve endangered species and create unforgettable experiences for visitors from around the world. What began as a small reserve has grown into a celebrated conservation hub.

Conservation Efforts

We work hand-in-hand with environmentalists to protect wildlife and maintain the natural beauty of our habitats. Every visit helps support breeding programs, anti-poaching patrols, and forest restoration.

Meet the Team

Our passionate team of guides, researchers, and conservationists are the heart of WildHaven. Their dedication ensures a safe, fun, and educational experience for every guest.
